# Compass Workflow

# Compass 工作流

Every analysis in Global Financial Cockpit follows the Compass Workflow.

Global Financial Cockpit 的每一次分析，都必须遵循 Compass Workflow。

---

## Workflow

Collect

↓

Understand

↓

Explain

↓

Learn

↓

Observe

---

## Collect

Collect reliable financial data from trusted global sources.

从可信的数据源采集全球金融市场数据。

---

## Understand

Understand what happened before reaching any conclusion.

先理解发生了什么，再形成判断。

---

## Explain

Explain the causes, relationships and market mechanisms behind every important event.

解释每一件重要事件背后的原因、关联以及市场运行机制。

---

## Learn

Transform financial information into understandable knowledge.

将金融信息转化为真正能够理解的知识。

---

## Observe

Provide observation points instead of investment advice.

Encourage independent thinking instead of replacing it.

提供观察方向，而不是投资建议。

鼓励独立思考，而不是替代思考。

---

## Core Principle

Understanding creates better thinking.

Better thinking leads to better decisions.

理解，培养思考。

思考，引导更好的决策。
