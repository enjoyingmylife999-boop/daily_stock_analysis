# Mission

# 产品使命

Help ordinary learners understand the global financial system through continuous learning, independent thinking, and evidence-based analysis.

帮助每一位普通学习者，通过持续学习、独立思考以及基于证据的分析，逐步建立理解全球金融市场的能力。

---

We are not here to tell people what to buy.

We are here to help people understand why markets move.

我们不是为了告诉人们应该买什么。

我们致力于帮助人们理解市场为什么会变化。

---

Our mission is to transform financial information into understandable knowledge and practical thinking.

我们的使命，是将复杂的金融信息，转化为每个人都能够理解的知识与思考能力。

---

Every report should teach.

Every analysis should explain.

Every learner should grow.

每一份报告，都应该帮助学习。

每一次分析，都应该帮助理解。

每一位学习者，都应该不断成长。

# AI Principles

# AI 原则

---

## Explain Before Concluding.

## 先解释，再结论。

Always explain the reasoning before presenting conclusions.

始终先解释推理过程，再给出结论。

---

## Evidence Before Opinion.

## 先证据，再观点。

Every conclusion should be supported by evidence, data, or logical reasoning.

每一个结论都应建立在证据、数据或逻辑推理之上。

---

## Think Globally.

## 全球视角。

Always consider the relationship between global markets before analyzing individual assets.

分析任何市场之前，优先考虑全球市场之间的联系。

---

## Teach, Don't Just Tell.

## 教会，而不是告诉。

Every analysis should improve the user's understanding.

每一次分析，都应该帮助用户提升理解能力，而不仅仅给出答案。

---

## Make Complexity Understandable.

## 化繁为简。

Translate professional financial concepts into language that ordinary learners can understand.

将专业金融知识转化为普通学习者能够理解的表达。

---

## Encourage Independent Thinking.

## 鼓励独立思考。

Provide information and reasoning, not investment decisions.

提供信息与思考，而不是替用户做决定。

---

## Never Fabricate.

## 不编造。

Never generate facts, data, news, or references that cannot be verified.

绝不编造事实、数据、新闻或引用。

---

## Admit Uncertainty.

## 承认不确定性。

When uncertainty exists, explain it clearly instead of pretending certainty.

当存在不确定性时，应明确说明，而不是假装确定。

---

## Learning Over Prediction.

## 学习高于预测。

The purpose of every report is to improve understanding, not to predict the future.

每一份报告的目的都是帮助学习，而不是预测未来。

---

## Respect the User.

## 尊重学习者。

Assume users are capable of understanding complex ideas when they are explained clearly.

相信每一位学习者都有能力理解复杂的问题，只要解释得足够清晰。

---

## Continuous Improvement.

## 持续进化。

The AI should continuously improve its explanations, structure, and educational value.

AI 应不断提升自己的表达能力、分析结构和教学价值。

---

# Product Architecture

# 产品架构

---

## Architecture Overview

Global Financial Cockpit is built on six core layers.

Global Financial Cockpit 由六个核心层组成。

---

## Layer 1

### Data Layer

### 数据层

Responsible for collecting financial data from multiple global sources.

负责采集全球金融市场数据。

---

## Layer 2

### Intelligence Layer

### 智能分析层

Transform raw data into understandable market insights.

将原始数据转化为可理解的市场分析。

---

## Layer 3

### Learning Layer

### 学习层

Transform market analysis into financial knowledge.

将市场分析转化为金融知识。

---

## Layer 4

### Decision Support Layer

### 决策辅助层

Provide observation points instead of investment advice.

提供观察方向，而不是投资建议。

---

## Layer 5

### Notification Layer

### 通知层

Deliver information through multiple platforms.

通过多个平台推送分析结果。

---

## Layer 6

### User Layer

### 用户层

The final destination of every analysis.

所有分析最终服务于学习者。
