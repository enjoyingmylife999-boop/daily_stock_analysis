# 1. Product Vision

# 1. 产品愿景

Financial markets are not something to predict.

They are something to understand.

---

金融市场不是一个需要预测的世界。

它是一个值得理解的系统。

---

Global Financial Cockpit is designed to help ordinary learners build a global perspective, understand financial markets, and develop independent thinking through continuous learning.

Global Financial Cockpit 致力于帮助每一位普通学习者建立全球视角，理解金融市场，并通过持续学习，逐步形成独立分析与独立思考的能力。

---

Our goal is not to tell people what to buy.

Our goal is to help people understand why markets move.

我们的目标不是告诉人们应该买什么。

而是帮助人们真正理解市场为什么会变化。

---

Every important event deserves a Why Chain.

每一件重要的财经事件，

都值得追问三个为什么。

---

🌍 Explore the world.

🧠 Understand the market.

📚 Keep learning.

探索世界。

理解市场。

持续学习。
